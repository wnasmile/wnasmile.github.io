About WannaSmile

WannaSmile is a personal web project that brings together a curated collection of tools, games, apps, and experiments — all accessible from one clean, themeable hub. It's built for speed, style, and a little fun.

All content is loaded dynamically from a Google Sheets database, meaning the library stays fresh without a full page rebuild. You can explore items by page, search, sort, favourite, or discover something random.

Use the keyboard shortcuts (→ / ← for pages, R to reload, Esc to panic-redirect), customise your theme in Settings, and mark your favourites with the ☆ star on any card.
